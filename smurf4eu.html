Project Overview

Building Europe's
AI Foundation

SMURF4EU addresses a critical gap in today's AI landscape: the lack of transparent, reproducible, and EU-language-capable alternatives to proprietary multimodal systems.

Building on the strong foundations of EuroLLM, the project will deliver multiple model sizes that support all 24 official EU languages, enabling multimodal reasoning through reinforcement learning and verifier-style rewards.

Beyond releasing model weights, SMURF4EU commits to open science — publishing checkpoints, datasets (where licensing permits), code, and full documentation, following best practices for safety and GDPR-aware data handling.

Core Research

Technical Goals

A modular, efficient architecture that makes it easy to add modalities to strong text models — while scaling to the lengths and contexts real-world multimodal tasks demand.

🧩

Modular Training Recipe

A clean architecture that allows new modalities to be added to strong text backbones via late fusion and shared token-like representations — without retraining from scratch.

⚡

Long-Context Scaling

Efficient attention and memory-compression techniques that scale to 1M-token multimodal contexts, enabling complex cross-modal reasoning over long documents, videos, and audio.

🧠

Multimodal Reasoning

Reasoning capabilities are developed through reinforcement learning and verifier-style reward signals, enabling models to reason across text, vision, code, speech, and video.

🌍

EU Multilingual Coverage

All 24 official EU languages are first-class targets, building on EuroLLM's multilingual expertise to ensure equitable performance across European linguistic communities.

🔬

Test-Time Scaling

Research into scaling compute at inference time — enabling models to "think harder" on difficult problems through structured search and iterative refinement strategies.

🛡️

Safety & Compliance

All models are developed following best practices for alignment, including GDPR-aware data handling, EU AI Act compliance, and responsible release procedures.
